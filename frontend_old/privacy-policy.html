Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

Shree Lakshmi Astro respects your privacy. This policy explains the information we collect and how we use it to provide astrology services.

Information we collect

  • Profile and contact details such as name, email, phone number, and login information.
  • Consultation information such as birth details, questions, selected service, and case notes.
  • Payment status and payment reference details required to verify transactions.

How we use information

We use your information to create consultation cases, verify payments, connect you with astrologers, provide support, and improve the service.

Sharing

We may share necessary consultation details with the assigned astrologer and payment details with payment gateway partners for transaction processing.

Contact

For privacy requests, please reach us through About & Contact.
