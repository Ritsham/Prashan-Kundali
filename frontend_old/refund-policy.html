Refund Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

Payments on Shree Lakshmi Astro are collected for astrology consultation services and related digital guidance.

Refund eligibility

  • If payment is deducted but the consultation case is not created, we will verify the payment and assist with service activation or refund.
  • If the astrologer is unavailable and no suitable appointment can be arranged, we may offer rescheduling or a refund after review.
  • Refunds are generally not applicable after the consultation has been fulfilled or the astrologer has provided the scheduled service.

Processing time

Approved refunds are processed through the original payment method. Bank and payment gateway timelines may vary.

Support

For refund help, please contact us through About & Contact with your payment reference and consultation details.
