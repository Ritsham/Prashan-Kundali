Join Our Astrologer Community

A private community for astrologers, practitioners, and serious students of astrology.

We are building an organic and trusted community where astrologers and serious students of astrology can discuss charts, share knowledge, study real cases, exchange interpretations, and learn from one another.

To maintain the quality of the community, access is not automatically provided to every registered user. We welcome practicing astrologers, astrology students, people learning under mentors, and individuals who are genuinely studying and practicing astrology.

We ask for some basic information about your astrology background and supporting proof so that we can understand your involvement in astrology and prevent unrelated users, spam accounts, and promotional accounts from entering the community.

You do not need to be a famous astrologer or have years of professional experience to apply. Genuine learners and practitioners are equally welcome.
