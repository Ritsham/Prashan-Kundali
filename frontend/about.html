About our platform

One astrology platform, two connected experiences

Astrology has been studied, practiced, and passed down through generations. Our platform brings digital astrology tools and a professional astrologer community together, so charts, questions, interpretations, discussions, and knowledge can stay organized and useful over time.

Prashna Kundali

Questions studied through the moment they arise

Prashna Kundali, also known as Horary Astrology, helps study a specific question using the exact moment and location at which the question is asked. It does not require birth details, and gives users a structured astrological framework for exploring important situations.

Lagna Kundali

Birth charts made easier to generate and explore

Lagna Kundali, or Birth Chart, is created from a person's date, exact time, and place of birth. The platform helps users generate and explore birth charts while preserving the depth needed for serious astrological interpretation.

Astro Community

A professional knowledge network for astrologers

Astro Community is built around persistent professional discussions, channel-organized posts, chart discussions, professional responses, and focused threads where astrologers can exchange interpretations and preserve valuable knowledge.

Our Vision

We believe technology should not replace astrologers. It should provide better tools for creating charts, studying cases, exchanging interpretations, discovering knowledge, and collaborating with other practitioners.
